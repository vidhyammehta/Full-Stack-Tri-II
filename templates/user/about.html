About Us

Who Are We

TeamUp! is a web application that helps people with the same interest in sports to network, have conversations and organise events.
We strive to provide a hassle-free transaction and a friendly and easy to understand user interface.

Our Mission

As a sports enthusiast company, we have made it our mission to encourage people to play sports outside of the internet, to keep their passion for their favourite games alive and to create a community where people can share their thoughts and have conversations.

Our Vision

TeamUp! can help users make new friends, socialise, and learn about new sports by connecting with others who are interested in sports.
